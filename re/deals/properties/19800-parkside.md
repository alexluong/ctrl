**Laundry:** Separate room with washer/dryer and large louvered closet. Nice to have.

**Enclosed porch/mudroom:** Vinyl flooring, exterior door. Functional transition space.

**Garage:** Detached, single car, with driveway access from rear. Older but standing. White panel door.

**Overall condition: 7/10.** Recently refreshed (paint, some flooring, bathroom) but not a full renovation. Baseboard heating throughout (no forced air — note: no central AC). Original bones with cosmetic updates. Move-in ready for a tenant.

## Concerns

- **3rd bedroom is NOT a real bedroom.** It's a wood-paneled attic with sloped ceilings. Market this as 2BR + bonus. Rent ceiling is $1,500 (2BR range), not $2,000 (3BR range).
- **Baseboard heating / no central AC.** Tenants may want AC in summer — window units will be needed. Not a dealbreaker but limits appeal slightly.
- **Vacancy gap.** Tenant out Apr 30 → need to find new tenant. Budget 1 month vacancy minimum. That's $1,500 out of pocket during turnover.
- **Sqft still unknown.** Need to confirm.
- **Exterior showing age.** Siding, porch, steps — nothing urgent but 3-5 year horizon items.
- **$129K is at the top of the metro buy box** ($130-150K). Not much room for error if things need work.

## My Analysis

This is the stronger of the two DSCR deals. The SCS location is a tier above Redford — better tenants, stronger appreciation trajectory, Mike's personal presence nearby. The tenant departure is actually a catalyst, not a problem — it was priced for investors with a below-market tenant, and now you get a clean slate to set market rent.

The risk is the unknowns: actual sqft, condition, that small 3rd bedroom. If it's really a 2BR in practice, the $1,500 rent still works (2BR in SCS go for $1,500+) but the $2,000 upside disappears.

**Key question: what are we offering? $129K full ask or negotiating down?** The previous tenant situation and the fact it's been listed "for the past month or so" (per Mike) give us leverage. Seller was stuck with a below-market tenant and investor-only buyer pool. I'd target $120-125K.
